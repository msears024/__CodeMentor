We Work for You!

Event Upload is built with love in Salt Lake City, Utah -- principally from the very coffee shops and venues that we serve.

We learned about the "event calendar" problem by trying to help local businesses market their upcoming events. As we found, the problem isn't a lack of event calendars; it's that there are too many and no single site or app to ensure that your customers will discover your event.

Thus, we automated the event upload experience -- promising to increase the reach of every event we upload and helping to ensure that people know the events happing where they live.
